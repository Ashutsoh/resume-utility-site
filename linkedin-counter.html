Why Character Count Matters on LinkedIn

LinkedIn truncates posts at around 140 characters in the feed, showing only the first few lines before the "see more" button. This makes your opening critical for engagement. Understanding character limits helps you craft content that displays properly and encourages interaction.

Posts between 1,300-2,000 characters tend to get the most engagement. They're long enough to provide value but short enough to maintain attention. Our tool helps you optimize your content length for maximum impact while staying within LinkedIn's technical limits.
