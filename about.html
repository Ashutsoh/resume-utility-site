About Resume Word Counter

Helping job seekers optimize resumes for ATS success

Our Mission

Resume Word Counter is a free online utility platform designed to help job seekers, professionals, and students optimize their resumes for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). We believe every candidate deserves a fair shot at landing their dream job.

Studies show that over 75% of resumes never reach human recruiters due to ATS filtering. Many qualified candidates get rejected not because of their skills, but because of improper resume length, formatting issues, or missing optimization best practices.

Why We Built This

After seeing countless talented professionals struggle with resume optimization, we created a simple solution: fast, accurate, and completely free tools that work instantly in your browser.

Our Philosophy: Resume optimization shouldn't require expensive software, complicated tutorials, or sharing your personal data. It should be instant, transparent, and accessible to everyone.

Recommended Resume Guidelines

Optimal Length: 400-600 words for entry-level positions, 600-800 words for mid-level, and up to 1000 words for senior/executive roles.

Page Count: One page for 0-5 years experience, two pages for 5-15 years, and up to three pages for executives with 15+ years.

Our tools help you stay within these ranges while maximizing impact and maintaining ATS compatibility.
