About Us

Resume Word Counter is a free online utility platform designed to help job seekers, professionals, and students optimize their resumes for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

Many resumes get rejected due to improper length, formatting issues, or missing best practices. Our goal is to provide fast, simple, and privacy-friendly tools that help users check resume word count, character length, and ATS-friendly limits instantly.

We focus on lightweight tools that work directly in the browser without requiring sign-ups or storing personal data. Our utilities are ideal for resumes, LinkedIn posts, and professional documents.

Our mission is to make resume optimization accessible, transparent, and easy for everyone.
